## Public Endpoints (Ink Sepolia)
To access Ink Sepolia, feel free to use one of the public endpoints below:

###### Gelato
    - HTTPS: `https://rpc-gel-sepolia.inkonchain.com`
    - Websocket: `wss://ws-gel-sepolia.inkonchain.com`

###### Quicknode
    - HTTPS: `https://rpc-qnd-sepolia.inkonchain.com/`
    - Websocket: `wss://rpc-qnd-sepolia.inkonchain.com`
